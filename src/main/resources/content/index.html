An Australian neighbourhood marketplace

Your bins have spare room. Your street can use it.

Bin Space keeps track of your council bin nights — and turns the empty half of your bin into something worth putting out. Neighbours with spare room take the extra bags of neighbours who've run out, and a little money changes hands.

How Bin Space works

Never miss bin night

Your council's collection schedule, with a reminder the night before and which lids to roll out — rubbish, recycling or garden.

Got spare room? Sell it

Bin half empty? List the spare room. A neighbour books it, drops their bag in before collection, and the money lands in your wallet.

Bin overflowing? Borrow some

Find spare room nearby and pay a neighbour to take your extra rubbish. Cheaper and greener than a trip to the tip.

Better with neighbours

Every booking is a small trade between people on the same streets — fewer overflowing bins, fewer tip runs, and a street that wastes less.

When you borrow room, you're paying a neighbour for the service of taking your extra rubbish to the kerb — nothing more complicated than that.

Opening suburb by suburb

The marketplace switches on in each suburb once enough locals have joined the waitlist. Sign up, share your invite link, and you'll see the progress bar move with every neighbour who signs up.
