Privacy Policy

Version 0.1 — effective 14 July 2026

Bin Space is operated by Great Southern Software Pty Ltd (ABN 11 700 174 787), an Australian company ("we", "us"). This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the Bin Space app and website, why we collect it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. We handle personal information under the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

The short version: we collect what the service needs to work and nothing more, we never sell personal information, there is no advertising or ad tracking in Bin Space, and you can delete your account from inside the app.

1. What we collect

  • Account details — your name, email address, and optionally a phone number, when you create an account (directly or via Google or Apple sign-in).
  • Addresses — the address(es) you register, so we can show your council collection schedule and place your listings on your street.
  • Listings and bookings — the listings you create, the bookings you make, and messages or reasons you submit (for example when reporting a problem).
  • Payment and payout information — wallet top-ups, bookings paid, and payouts. Card details go directly to Stripe, our payment processor; we never see or store your card number. If you sell bin space, Stripe collects identity and bank details from you to meet its legal (KYC) obligations.
  • Device and notification data — a push-notification token for your device if you enable reminders, and basic technical logs (IP address, app version, timestamps) generated by using the service.
  • Location — only if you grant it, to suggest nearby listings. You can use Bin Space fully without granting location access.

2. How we use it

To provide the service: showing your bin schedule, running the marketplace, processing payments and payouts, sending the notifications you ask for, keeping the neighbourhood marketplace safe (fraud and abuse prevention, moderation of reported content), and meeting our legal obligations (tax and financial record-keeping).

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising. There are no ad networks or advertising trackers in the app or on our websites.

3. Who sees your address

Your exact address is shown to the other party of a booking only after the booking is confirmed between you. Before that, browsers of the marketplace see only an approximate location (street or suburb level).

4. Who we share information with

Only the service providers that make Bin Space work:

  • Google (Firebase Authentication, Cloud services, Firebase Cloud Messaging) — sign-in, hosting and push notifications.
  • Stripe (Stripe Payments Australia Pty Ltd and its affiliates) — payments, wallets and seller payouts, including Stripe's own identity verification for sellers.
  • Professional advisers or authorities where the law requires it.

Some of these providers process data outside Australia (principally the United States). We rely on their contractual safeguards and certified security programmes when they do (APP 8).

5. How long we keep it

Account information is kept while your account is active. Records of transactions (bookings, wallet movements, payouts) are kept for seven years after they occur, as Australian tax and financial law requires — even if you delete your account. Everything else is deleted or de-identified when no longer needed.

6. Delete your account

You can delete your account at any time from inside the app: menu → Delete account. Before deletion you'll be asked to resolve anything still open — active listings or bookings, or money left in your wallet (pay it out or spend it) — so nobody is left out of pocket.

When you delete your account we remove your name, email, phone number, addresses and sign-in identity, and disable the account permanently. Transaction records are retained in de-identified form for the legal retention period above.

If you can't access the app, email support@bin-space.app from your account email and we'll do it for you within 30 days.

7. Your other rights

You may ask us for access to the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct it (most of it you can view and edit directly in the app). Email support@bin-space.app — we respond within 30 days.

8. Security

Traffic is encrypted in transit (TLS). Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure with access limited to what operating the service requires. Payment card data never touches our systems (it goes directly to Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 provider).

9. Age

Bin Space involves payments between neighbours, so it is for people 18 and over. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

10. Complaints

If you think we've mishandled your personal information, email support@bin-space.app and we'll respond within 30 days. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (oaic.gov.au).

11. Changes

If we change this policy in a way that matters, we'll tell you in the app before the change takes effect and keep earlier versions available. Non-material clarifications take effect when published.
